Frequently Asked Questions

What are generic medicines?
Generic medicines contain the same active ingredients as branded medicines and are equally effective but cost significantly less.
Are generic medicines safe?
Yes, generic medicines are approved by regulatory authorities and undergo the same rigorous testing as branded medicines.
How do you find similar medicines?
We analyze the composition of your medicine and find alternatives with identical or similar active ingredients.
Is my prescription data secure?
We use end-to-end encryption and comply with healthcare data privacy regulations to ensure your data remains confidential.
