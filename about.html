About Us

Making healthcare affordable and accessible for everyone

Our Mission

To democratize access to affordable healthcare by bridging the information gap between consumers and generic medicine alternatives, ensuring no one has to choose between health and financial stability.

Our Vision

To become India's most trusted platform for medicine information and affordable healthcare solutions, saving families billions annually on medical expenses while maintaining treatment quality.

Our Story

GenMed was founded in 2023 by a team of healthcare professionals, pharmacists, and technology experts who noticed a critical gap in the Indian healthcare system.

While working in various hospitals and pharmacies, our founders observed that many patients were paying 5-10 times more for branded medicines when equally effective generic alternatives were available.

The challenge was that consumers lacked accessible information about these alternatives and their compositions. Doctors often prescribed branded medicines due to familiarity, and patients trusted these recommendations without knowing about cheaper options.

We built GenMed to solve this problem - creating a platform where anyone can easily find generic alternatives to their prescribed medicines, compare compositions, check similarity percentages, and make informed decisions about their healthcare spending.
